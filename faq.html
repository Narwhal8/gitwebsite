This (as of yet unnamed game) Is simply a passion project by me. It is my first major solidity project and I wanted to make an on-chain game where EVERYTHING was on-chain, not just some of the mechanics.

What are the features?

Great Question! The current feature list includes:
  • The ability to pick from one of 9 Kingdom Types, and add some of the over 30 Land types to your demense
  • Develope your kingdom with upgradeable housing & industry to produce some of hundreds of goods to either increase your productivity or sell to the market for what you lack
  • Employ your population to grow some of the dozens of different crops on your land, extract what is naturally on it for unique reasources, or invite your Nobility to hunt it for lucractive game
  • Build an army up of soliders you personally equip in a highly customizable system, or hire mercenaries (merc hiring/prices still under development) to engage in PvE quests for exclusive rewards
  • With more to come as development progesses!

How do I get started?

    Great question! The first step is to pick a kingdom type (Go to select action -> Set Kingdom Type) and aquire the respective type of land (If you are playing on the testnet build, simply ask for some). If you have any questions about which land does what you can either consult the game encylopedia or simply ask.

   Second, it is recomended that you build some housing (Go to select action -> Build -> Land Permit) so you have people to develope the land. This process will use 20 land of your choosing from your wallet per house

   Third is add some of your land directy to your kingdom (Go to select action -> Add Land To Kingdom) This land will now be workable. It is best to reserve some land as other buildings such as Cookeries that can be built later need a few land and cant use land already added to the kingdom for this purpose

   Fourth, you can finally get to reasource production now! (Go to select action -> Work Land). You can either farm crops by clicking on one, or get "Natural Reasources" from the land such as wood and stone to build your kingdom. You can also use food to increase the yields from your land by adding it in the box labeled "What would you like to feed your workers". This step is however optional and when you start out you will not have any additional food

What do the seasons do?

The effect of each season is as follows:
Winter: Temprature -20
Spring: No change
Summer: Temprature + 20
Fall: Temprature - 10
